Our Story


Who We Are?

Trans Globe, established in 1992 at Rajkot, Gujarat is one of the oldest and trusted study abroad education consultancy which partners with universities and institutions in Australia, New Zealand, UK, US, Canada. Our friendly and professional counsellors help students to identify, apply and secure admissions and visas at universities and countries where they can study the degree of their choice and flourish. We are member of AAERI supported by AEI of Australian High Commission with a network of 9 offices all over Gujarat and in Australia.We offer authentic counselling with strong research, simplified process, select global destinations, universities, colleges and courses.
