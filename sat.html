SCHOLASTIC ASSESSMENT TEST

SAT stands for ‘Scholastic Assessment test’ and is an entrance test for students aspiring to apply for undergraduate courses abroad, especially in the US, Canada & Singapore Universities. It is a written paper-pencil test and helps evaluate the language and mathematical reasoning skills of the applicants. Also, the universities you apply to, also take into consideration your SAT scores when you apply for admission. SAT essay is optional for the USA.


Reading :Evaluation of your communication skills.
Writing and Language : Your writing skills are evaluated and so the grip on the language through your use of words
Math—No Calculator : Basics! It is done to introspect your IQ.
Math—Calculator : It involves a few concepts that are necessary for the evaluation to prove yourself a perfect fit. It involves data analysis and problem solving abilities.
